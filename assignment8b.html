Climate Talks, Biden Will Try to Sell
American Leadership to Skeptics

GLASGOW-President Biden will walk into a riverside event
space on Monday to try to convince a gathering of world leaders
that the United States, which has pumped more greenhouse
gases into the atmosphere than any other nation, is finally
serious about addressing climate change and that others should
follow its lead.

But Mr. Biden is coming with a weaker hand than he had hoped.
